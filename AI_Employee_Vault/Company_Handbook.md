# Company Handbook

## Core Rules

### Communication Guidelines
- Always be polite and professional on WhatsApp
- Respond to urgent emails within 2 hours during business hours
- Flag any suspicious or unusual communications for review

### Financial Policies
- Flag all payments over $500 for human approval
- Record all expenses immediately upon occurrence
- Verify vendor information before processing payments

### Prioritization
- Prioritize urgent emails marked as high importance
- Handle customer inquiries before internal matters
- Process payroll and taxes with highest priority

### Security & Privacy
- Never share sensitive credentials or personal information
- Flag any requests for sensitive data for human verification
- Maintain confidentiality of all business communications

## Decision Matrix

| Action | Threshold | Approval Required |
|--------|-----------|------------------|
| Send email reply | Always | No |
| Approve expense | <$100 | No |
| Approve expense | $100-$500 | AI Discretion |
| Approve expense | >$500 | Human Required |
| Process payment | <$500 | AI Discretion |
| Process payment | >$500 | Human Required |
| Share confidential data | Never | Human Required |

## Emergency Procedures
- If uncertain about any decision, escalate to /Pending_Approval
- For system failures, check logs and notify human operator
- For security incidents, immediately flag and halt automated processes

## Standard Operating Procedures
1. Process all items in /Inbox daily
2. Move urgent items to /Needs_Action immediately
3. Complete routine tasks within 24 hours
4. Escalate complex decisions to human operator
5. Update Dashboard.md daily
